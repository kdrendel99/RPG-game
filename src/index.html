Moonscape

Will you reach the end?

Welcome, traveler! It appears you believe you've got what it takes to make it through this harsh, dangerous Moonscape terrain! I propose we find out if you're correct or not...
Your goal is simple: Make it to the end of the Moonscape terrain. Your character bar will indicate to you how your character is doing. Each turn you 'walk' without running into a monster, you gain one point of progress. Once you reach 20, you have successfully navigated your way to safety. The higher you level up, the more damage you'll do with a critical hit to your enemy.
Beware though, traveler. Many have failed before you. One knows not what lies around each corner!
